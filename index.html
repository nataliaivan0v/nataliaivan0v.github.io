Proviras

Cost optimization for AI agents

Many agentic workflows route every step to the most expensive frontier models, even when the simpler, cheaper models could handle the task just as well.

Proviras drops into a LangGraph flow via SDK, captures every LLM call, and shadow-tests smaller models against real production traffic to show teams exactly where they can safely downgrade, with measured equivalence rates and side-by-side examples. I built and shipped both the TypeScript and Python SDKs (on npm and PyPI).
